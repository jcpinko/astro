General:
This is the lab associated with Physics 234, Mechanics. You will have an informational meeting on the first week where you will be assigned your first lab.

Text: You need to buy the "Experiments in Mechanics" lab mansual.  It can be purchased in the Physics Office, Science Annex 202, from Secretary Sherry Murino (afternoons).  It costs $10.  Please bring exact change.

Grading:
 
Your final letter grade is determined based on your Lab Report scores.  The scores are determined by your TA with guidance (a rubrik) from your instructor. 

Course Policies

Attendance  is essential for labs.  You will not be able to make up missed labs unless you have a valid excuse, such as an activity with a music or sports group, or an emergency that can be proven (within reason).

Calculators. I encourage you to have a calculator in this lab.

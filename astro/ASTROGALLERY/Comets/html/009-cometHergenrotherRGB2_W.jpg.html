Comet Hergenrother taken with the Meade DSI on the 12" LX200. The exposure was a total of > 300 seconds made by tracking on the Comet's nucleus and summing up numerous ~15 second exposures. The file was saved in FITS format, RGBL, and recombined into one color picture with SAO DS9.

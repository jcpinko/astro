The full moon rising over the ONU campus, during the March 19, 2011 "supermoon". This happens when the full moon occurs at perigee, and the Moon is closest to the Earth in its orbit. The March 19 supermoon appeared almost 20% brighter and 15% larger than the average full moon. Taken with Canon Digital Rebel XT.

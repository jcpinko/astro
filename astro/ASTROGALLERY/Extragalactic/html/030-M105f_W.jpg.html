A group of galaxies known as the M96 Group. M96 is the galaxy on the lower left, while M105 is the brightest galaxy on the right. The image also contains NGC 3384 and NGC 3389. Although also a prominent member of the group, M95 is not visible in this image. Taken with Atik 343L CCD and Meade 80mm apochromatic refractor.

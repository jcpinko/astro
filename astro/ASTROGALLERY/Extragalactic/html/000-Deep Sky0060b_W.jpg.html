M101, also known as the Pinwheel Galaxy. This spiral galaxy is of type Sc. The bright "star" on the right edge of M101 is SN 2011fe, a supernova that reached apparent magnitude +10 at its brightest. DSI II CCD, Meade 80mm apochromatic refractor.

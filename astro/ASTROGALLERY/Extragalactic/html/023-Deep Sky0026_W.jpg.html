M51, the Whirlpool Galaxy. This spiral galaxy is unusual in that it is interacting with another dwarf galaxy nearby, giving rise to the name. Although faint, also visible is supernova SN 2011dh. The galaxy is of type Sbc. DSI II CCD, Meade 80mm apochromatic refractor.

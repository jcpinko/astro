The Flame Nebula and Horsehead Nebula are two nebulae in the Orion Molecular Complex. The Flame Nebula is in the lower center, and the Horsehead Nebula is to the center right. The bright blue star in the image is Alnitak, the leftmost star on Orion's belt. Taken with an Atik 343L CCD and an 80mm Meade apochromatic refractor.

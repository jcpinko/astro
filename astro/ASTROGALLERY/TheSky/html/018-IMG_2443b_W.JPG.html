The constellation Orion, with the corner of the ONU Observatory helpfully pointing to the bright blue star Rigel. Also visible are Orion's belt of three stars, in the center; Betelgeuse, a red supergiant in the center top of the photo; Sirius, the bright star on the left in Canis Major; the Orion Nebula, visible as a group of stars making up Orion's "sword" under his belt; and Taurus, a constellation shaped like a V and marked by the bright orange star Aldebaran on the right. Taken with Canon Digital Rebel XT.

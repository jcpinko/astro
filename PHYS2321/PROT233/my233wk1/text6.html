Ground

  • Something you stand on…

  • A very large conductor where like charges (that repel) can separate over large distances and be happy

    • large conductor – you do not give it a significant amount of charge
    • Earth is considered GROUND
    • When a charged object is grounded, it becomes neutral “instantly”
      • Ex: electric discharge on a door knob
        • The door knob is “ground”

  • Humidity

    • Air is more conductive
      • Objects discharge over time

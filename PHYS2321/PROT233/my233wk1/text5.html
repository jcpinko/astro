Electrical Conduction

  • Electrical conductivity is a property of a material

    • Ability to transmit electrical charge
    • Depends on bond strength of outer electrons
      • loosely or tightly bound

  • Conductors

    • Metals (electrons very mobile)
      • valance electrons easily move
    • Water with ions

  • Semi-conductors

  • Insulators (poor conductors)

    • Takes a long time for charge to move around
    • Plastics, pure water, rubber

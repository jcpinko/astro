Advice on homework
  • Be complete!
  • Be correct.
  • Show work - don't try to do it all "in your head".
  • Be neat. Circle or box in final answers. Put a space or line between problems.
  • Don't leave a "frill" on the pages. (Engineering paper works well.)
  • Don't copy without understanding.
  • Bring questions to class or office hours.

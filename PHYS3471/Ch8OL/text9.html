Boltzmann Factor

  • Fundamental idea used in all branches of physics

  • The higher the energy of a state, the less likely it will be occupied

    • For the Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution, the energy is Kinetic Energy

  • The “kT” term is associated with the thermal energy of the “gas” as a whole

  • Ratio of Probabilities for two different states (and energies)

Saha Equation

  • Determines the ratio of ionized atoms

  • Need partition functions since all the atoms are not in the same state

    • Zi is the initial stage of ionization
    • Zi+1 is the final stage of ionization

  • Ratio of the number of atoms in each of these stages

    • ne is the electron density (an ideal gas of electrons)
      • Electron pressure
      • Electrons recombine with H II to give H I
